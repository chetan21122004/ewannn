The 5 Operational Gaps

01

Regulatory & Entity Structuring

Choosing the wrong entity type (e.g., branch vs. subsidiary) can trigger irreversible tax liabilities and restrict specific operational licenses in the target jurisdiction.

02

Partner & Distribution Control

Over-reliance on local distributors without clear exit clauses or IP protection frameworks leads to brand hijacking and margin erosion.

03

The "Coordination Tax"

The invisible cost of misalignment between HQ assumptions and local vendor realities, causing project timelines to stretch from months to years.

04

Human Capital Deployment

Visa delays, localized benefits compliance, and misjudging the cultural expectations of top-tier local talent.

05

Executive Liaison Friction

Lack of a localized authority figure to translate board directives into immediate on-the-ground action, leading to decision paralysis.
